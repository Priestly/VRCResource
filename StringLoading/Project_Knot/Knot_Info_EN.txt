Someone lives alone in a house on a hill.
They open the window at hours you never see, breathe air you have never known, and wait quietly for you to return.

Though you share the same body, you can never meet.
Even the color of the sky above you must be different.

In a small palace filled with flowers and shimmering reflections on the water, there is a single letter.
It is addressed to you, from "the other me."
